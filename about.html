Brief Biography

Dr. Durgesh Nandan did his Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) at the topic “Design and analysis of an efficient architecture of logarithmic multiplier and its applications” from Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering, Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology, Guna, Madhya Pradesh, India in year 2018 with the specialization in VLSI. Before completion of his doctorate, he did his Master of Technology with honours from Rajeev Gandhi Technical University, Madhya Pradesh, India in Electronics & Communication Engineering with specialization Microelectronics & VLSI Design in year 2013. He completed his Graduation with Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Rajeev Gandhi Technical University, Madhya Pradesh, India in year 2009. He found prestigious “JSS fellowship” for duration of 2014 to 2018 by Jayaprakash Sewa Sasthan.

He served as Assistant professor and Head of department in the Department of Electronics and communication Engineering, IASSCOM fortune institutes of technology, India, from March 2013 to July 2014. He served as a Guest faculty under SMDP-C2SD in the Department of Electronics and communication Engineering, National Institutes of Technology (NIT), Patna, India, from April 2018 to September 2018. Currently worked as a role of the Research Mentor in CL educate Ltd from September 2018.

He is TPC/reviewer member of more than 50 ruptured national/international conference proceedings. He is the author or a co-author of more than 100 papers, which was published SCI, Scopus and peer reviewed international journals and conference proceedings. His research interest’s extent in number of areas like computer arithmetic, VLSI architecture for signal processing applications, Quantum computing and Hardware architecture of real time big data/ AI applications.
